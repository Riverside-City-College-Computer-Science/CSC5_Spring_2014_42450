                                                   BULLS AND COWS
                                                   --------------
In this game, the computer and you are going to think of a random  4-digit number. Both of you will try to guess each others number.
You will first guess the computers number.
To win, guess the computers number in less tries than the computer.
You go first.
Follow the computers hints: 
Bulls = correct digit, in a correct position.
Cows = correct digit, in an incorrect position.
